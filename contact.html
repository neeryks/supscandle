Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, choose your favorite candle, and click "Buy Now via Instagram." This will open Instagram DM with a pre-filled message. Simply send it, and we'll confirm your order details and arrange payment and delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and major mobile payment apps. Payment details will be shared via Instagram DM after you place your order.

How long do the candles burn?

Our candles are designed for long-lasting enjoyment. Small candles burn for 25-30 hours, medium for 40-45 hours, and large for 60+ hours with proper care.

Are your candles eco-friendly?

Absolutely! We use 100% natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and phthalate-free fragrances. Our packaging is recyclable and minimal to reduce environmental impact.

Do you offer shipping?

Yes, we ship nationwide. Shipping costs and delivery times will be confirmed when you place your order via Instagram DM.

Can I create a custom candle?

We love custom orders! Message us on Instagram with your ideas for scent, size, or special packaging, and we'll work with you to create something perfect.
